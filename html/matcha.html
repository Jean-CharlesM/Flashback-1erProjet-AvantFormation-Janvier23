Imperial Matcha

Description


Imperial matcha is an organic green tea from Japan, tonic, round and mellow, which develops beautiful vegetal notes, enhanced by a powerful umami flavor. It is made with the greatest care, from tencha leaves: 3 weeks before harvest, the tea plants are deprived of most of the light so that their leaves develop incomparable flavors. These tencha leaves are then delicately ground as finely as flour. It is a tea that is not infused, but is mixed with water using a whisk. Thus, the tea leaves are absorbed directly, which explains the delicious taste of Imperial matcha, as well as its many benefits. It is a real superfood with many virtues. Rich in caffeine, with energizing properties and in theanine which promotes relaxation and concentration, a bowl of matcha would contain up to 3 times more catechin than any other green tea (molecule with strong antioxidant power). This tea comes from organic farming and has been certified by Ecocert, an approved independent inspection body.



Suggested preparation


Unlike all other teas, matcha is a tea whose leaves have been reduced to a very fine powder. This green tea is therefore not infused but beaten. It is prepared using a whisk, bowl and spatula :


  • Put 2 spatulas (i.e. 1 level teaspoon) of Imperial matcha in the bowl.
  • Pour 7cl of water heated to 70Â°C over the powder.
  • Holding the bowl firmly with one hand, beat the tea using the whisk, with a jerky movement until you obtain a well-emulsified foam. Once the matcha is frothy, sip it.
