Genmaicha Yama

Description


Genmaicha Yama is a traditional Japanese tea, a mixture of Bancha green tea and roasted brown rice. The fresh vegetal notes of the tea combine harmoniously with the roasted ones of the cereals, and give it a very characteristic taste which makes it a very popular tea for those who are not accustomed to green tea. Very thirst-quenching, this surprising tea can be enjoyed both hot and iced, and goes particularly well with your meals.



Suggested preparation


Prepare this hot tea :
Take 6 g of tea leaves for 30 cl of water and heat to 75°. Infuse for 3 minutes.
Prepare this iced tea:
Infuse 15 g of tea per liter of water at room temperature for 30 minutes. Remove the leaves and place in the fridge.
Prepare this tea latte:
Infuse 8 g in a mixture of 25 cl of water at 75°C and 25 cl of hot milk (animal or vegetable) for 5 min. Froth using the electric whisk.


Ingredients


Green tea (Japan) (52%), roasted brown rice (48%)
