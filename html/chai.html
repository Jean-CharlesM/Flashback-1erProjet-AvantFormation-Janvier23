Imperial Chaï

Description


At the crossroads of Indian influences and French refinement, discover this exclusive creation. The Chaï Impérial (pronounced "chaï") is distinguished by its blend of precious spices such as cardamom, cinnamon or pink peppercorns. Sprinkled with a mixture of spices called "massala", it is a tea with gourmet and warm notes that can be enjoyed plain or with milk, as is the Indian tradition.



Suggested preparation


Prepare this hot tea :
Take 6 g of tea leaves for 30 cl of water and heat to 75°. Infuse for 3 minutes.
Prepare this iced tea:
Infuse 15 g of tea per liter of water at room temperature for 30 minutes. Remove the leaves and place in the fridge.
Prepare this tea latte:
Infuse 8 g in a mixture of 25 cl of water at 75°C and 25 cl of hot milk (animal or vegetable) for 5 min. Froth using the electric whisk.


Ingredients


Black tea (China) (52%), ginger, orange peel, cardamom (5%), cinnamon (4%), clove, pink peppercorns (3%), natural cinnamon flavouring, safflower petals, flavouring.
