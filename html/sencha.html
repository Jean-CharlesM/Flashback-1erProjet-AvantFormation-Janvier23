Sencha Ariake

Description


This Japanese green tea is a very good introduction to the diversity of Sencha, the most consumed tea family in Japan. Steamed then folded into needles, these teas have vegetal and marine notes. The Sencha Ariake is the most accessible of the Sencha. It is produced in the province of Shizuoka. Both soft and present in the mouth, it is ideal in the morning or at the beginning of the afternoon. It is also a tea rich in antioxidants! Suggested preparation


Prepare this hot tea :
Take 6 g of tea leaves for 30 cl of water and heat to 75°. Infuse for 3 minutes.
Prepare this iced tea:
Infuse 15 g of tea per liter of water at room temperature for 30 minutes. Remove the leaves and place in the fridge.
Prepare this tea latte:
Infuse 8 g in a mixture of 25 cl of water at 75°C and 25 cl of hot milk (animal or vegetable) for 5 min. Froth using the electric whisk.
