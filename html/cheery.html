Cheery tree

Description


Cheery tree is one of the most popular teas in our range. Gourmet and fruity, Cheery Tree is inspired by a traditional Turkish recipe. Its fragrance evokes green dates, orange blossom, roses and red fruits. It is embellished, in the purest oriental tradition, with flower petals. Its extraordinary fragrance is born from the subtle combination of green tea from China, renowned for its freshness and thirst-quenching properties, and the delicious flavors of fruit.



Suggested preparation


Prepare this hot tea :
Take 6 g of tea leaves for 30 cl of water and heat to 75°. Infuse for 3 minutes.
Prepare this iced tea:
Infuse 15 g of tea per liter of water at room temperature for 30 minutes. Remove the leaves and place in the fridge.
Prepare this tea latte:
Infuse 8 g in a mixture of 25 cl of water at 75°C and 25 cl of hot milk (animal or vegetable) for 5 min. Froth using the electric whisk.


Ingredients


Green tea (China) (94%), flavorings (including strawberry 2%), flower petals (mauve, red rose, sunflower) (3%).
