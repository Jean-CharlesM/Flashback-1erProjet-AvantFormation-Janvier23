Yunann Imperial

Description


Flowery and honeyed, the great character and the subtlety of this tea have earned it the name of "Mocha of teas" or "tea of ​​surgeons", because it wakes you up without getting upset. Superb leaf, with lots of golden buds. One of the "must" of Palais des Thés. The Imperial Grand Yunnan is the most popular single-origin tea at Palais des Thés. It has converted a large number of coffee drinkers and is a good replacement for coffee at the start of the day.



Suggested preparation


Prepare this hot tea :
Take 6 g of tea leaves for 30 cl of water and heat to 75°. Infuse for 3 minutes.
Prepare this iced tea:
Infuse 15 g of tea per liter of water at room temperature for 30 minutes. Remove the leaves and place in the fridge.
Prepare this tea latte:
Infuse 8 g in a mixture of 25 cl of water at 75°C and 25 cl of hot milk (animal or vegetable) for 5 min. Froth using the electric whisk.


Ingredients


Black tea (China).
